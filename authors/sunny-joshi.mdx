**Sunny Joshi** is the Creative Director and Lead UI/UX Designer at OnCall. He ensures that our debugging tools are not just powerful, but intuitive and aesthetically refined.

With over 9 years of experience, Sunny creates user-centric solutions that combine creativity with measurable business impact. His work spans Fortune 500 companies, global brands, and fast-growing startups.

### Accolades & Background

- **Awards:** Work recognized at Cannes Lions, Effies, Kyoorius, and ET Shark Awards.
- **Philosophy:** Designing experiences that feel effortless, look beautiful, and deliver results.
