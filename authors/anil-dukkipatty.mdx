**Anil Dukkipatty** is the Distributed Systems Architect at OnCall. He leads the engineering strategy for high-throughput log ingestion and fault tolerance.

With over 10 years of experience, Anil has built core blockchain infrastructure using Raft, P2P gossip protocols, and Solidity. He was an early contributor to **TMSP** (the networking layer of Tendermint, now Cosmos).

### Accolades & Background

- **Elemential Labs:** Led the architecture of blockchain systems that passed rigorous security audits for **NSE**, **NSDL**, **CAMS**, and **ICICI**.
- **Razorpay:** Serves as an AI Advisor.
- **Deep Tech:** Expert in consensus algorithms and distributed state machines.
