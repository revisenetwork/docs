**Raunaq Vaisoha** is a Co-Founder at OnCall. A serial entrepreneur in the Web3 and developer tooling space, he specializes in building systems that handle high-velocity dynamic data.

Raunaq previously co-founded **Revise**, where he raised \$3.5M to solve data structure issues for dynamic NFTs. He also set up the blockchain lab at the **National Stock Exchange of India (NSE)**, working with SEBI and RBI on industry-first applications.

### Accolades & Background

- **HackCoin:** Founded in 2015, hosting hackathons for 500+ developers.
- **CoinTelegraph:** Started his career as their youngest staff writer.
- **Revise:** Backed by Alpha Wave Global, Polygon founders, and AngelList India.
